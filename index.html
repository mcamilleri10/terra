The Representative Concentration Pathway (RCP) is a greenhouse gas concentration trajectory adopted by the IPCC. RCP4.5 describes a scenario in which emissions peak around the year 2040, resulting in a global temperature rise of 2 - 3°C. RCP8.5 describes a scenario in which emissions continue to increase, resulting in a global temperature rise of 3 - 5°C - considered a worst-case scenario.
